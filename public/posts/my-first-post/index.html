Simple LED Circuit Introduction A simple LED circuit is one of the most basic electronic circuits. It consists of an LED, a resistor, and a power source (battery or adapter). This circuit is great for beginners to learn the basics of electricity and circuits.

Step-by-Step Instructions Connect the positive terminal of the battery to one leg of the resistor (220Œ© or 330Œ©).

Connect the other leg of the resistor to the anode (long leg) of the LED.

Connect the cathode (short leg) of the LED to the negative terminal of the battery.

Turn on the power and see the LED glow!

Working Principle The resistor controls the amount of current flowing through the LED.

If too much current flows, the LED can burn out.

The resistor prevents this by limiting the current to a safe level.
